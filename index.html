ABOUT US

Welcome to the official website of International Conference on Current Trends in Materials Science and Engineering 2021 (CTMSE 2021). CTMSE 2021, organised by Institute of Engineering & Management, Kolkata, India will be held in online platform, during 11th – 13th March, 2021. After the successful execution of CTMSE 2018 and CTMSE 2019, CTMSE 2021 is to bring together innovative academicians and industrial experts in the field of Materials Science and Engineering. The conference will address advancements currently witnessed in the fields of Computational Materials, Smart Materials, Graphene and other 2D Materials, Carbon Nanostructures and Devices, Semiconductor Devices, Magnetism and Spintronics, Nanomaterials and Nanotechnology, Multifunctional Materials, Polymers, composite and ceramic materials, Environmental and Green Materials, Nano biotechnology and Nanomedicine. The conference aspires to exhibit the research and technical excellence of scientists and technocrats, representatives from the academia and industry. This conference aims to bring together the best of globally renowned research professionals. It will include talks and keynote lectures delivered by eminent speakers besides scientific paper presentations and poster contributions. We hope and believe that our international conference ‘CTMSE 2021’ will prove to be a great learning opportunity and an enriching experience for all the participants. English is the official language of the conference. We welcome paper submissions. Papers presented in the conference will be submitted for inclusion into Conference Abstract Booklet (digital copy). Full papers submitted will be published in AIP Conference Proceedings or International Journal of Innovative Research in Physics

Call for Papers 

CTMSE is a platform for the presentation of new advances and research results in the fields of materials science and engineering. The conference will bring together leading scientists, researchers and scholars in their domain of interest in Materials science and engineering from around the world. Topics of interest for submission include, but not limited to.

Computational Materials 

    Smart Materials

    Engineering application of Materials

    Emerging Materials and Applications

    Graphene and other 2D Materials

    Carbon Nanostructures and Devices

    Nanobiotechnology

Semiconductor Devices 

    Magnetic Nanostructures

    Nanomaterials and Nanotechnology

    Multifunctional Materials

    Polymers, Composite and Ceramic materials

    Environmental and Green Materials

    Nano Medicine

All full length papers submitted will be peer reviewed. Selected full length papers after peer review will be published. 

PUBLICATION

Full papers submitted will be peer reviewed and published in AIP Conference Proceedings/ International Journal of Innovative Research in Physics (IJIIP) Published by Smart Society, USA (Print ISSN : 2689-484X, online ISSN: 2687-7902)

Submission

Abstract Submission Guidelines

  • Abstract should be prepared in Microsoft Word.
  • Please download the abstract template from the website.
  • The deadline for abstract submission is 30/10/2020. No abstracts will be accepted after this date.
  • Abstracts MUST be prepared in English.
  • Abstracts should not exceed 250 words.(Text only).
  • Maximum one figure and two references are allowed in the abstract.
Note:

Full length papers submission is optional. Those who wants to participate and do not want publication, do not need to submit full length paper. Those who wish to opt for publication in international journal/conference proceedings with ISSN number should submit full length paper of maximum 8 pages (including figures and references) within due date.Full papers submitted will be published in AIP Conference Proceedings/ International Journal of Innovative Research in Physics (IJIIP) Published by Smart Society, USA (Print ISSN : 2689-484X, online ISSN: 2687-7902) Full paper should be written in the template given below in the website.

Please ensure that your e-mail address (ID) and mobile number is accurate at the time of submission. All correspondence will be sent via e-mail to the author. Should the author's e-mail address change after the submission deadline, please notify the convener at ctmse@iemcal.com to update this information. All presenters of selected abstracts are required to register and pay the appropriate registration fees.

Abstracts may be submitted for Oral or Poster Presentation.
